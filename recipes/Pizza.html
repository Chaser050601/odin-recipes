Pizza Recipe

Worlds Best Pizza

Tangy pizza sauce with a thin crispy crust and fresh mozzarella cheese

Ingredients

  • Cento canned tomatoes
  • Mozzarella
  • Fresh basil
  • Pizza dough
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Make pizza dough 6 hours to 3 days before hand
  2. Preheat oven to 550f
  3. In blender blend tomatoes,oil,basil,salt,and pepper
  4. stretch out dough to appropriate size
  5. build your pizza how you want
  6. put in oven for about 8-12 minutes
