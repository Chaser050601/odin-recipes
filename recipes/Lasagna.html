Lasagna Recipe

Worlds Best Homemade Lasagna

This is a rich tomato sauce lasagna recipe passed down generation to generation in my family now im going to share it with y'all

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Basil
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. In a large pot put olive oil, Onion, and Garlic on medium heat
  2. while doing that heat up pot of water salted like the ocean
  3. Once onions are translucent and garlic is cooked down a little put in the tomatoes
  4. Once water is to a boil add the pasta until al denta
  5. Once tomatoes are broken down hand blend them and let the tomato sauce reduce
  6. once everything is done layer everything
    1. sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Sauce
    4. Cheese
    5. Repeat
  7. Bake in oven at 350f for 45 min then let sit for 30 min before serving
