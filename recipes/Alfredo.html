Alfredo Recipe

Homemade Alfredo Pasta

Rich and creamy alfredo pasta

Ingredients

  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Parmesan
  • Garlic
  • Fettuccine
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. In a pan heat down the butter and put in the garlic until the bitterness of it is gone
  2. While doing that heat a pot of water until boiling with salt in it
  3. and boil the Fettuccine until al denta
  4. Once the garlic is done add heavy cream and slowly stir in a little cheese at a time
  5. once fully incorporated add the pasta with some pasta water and season to liking
